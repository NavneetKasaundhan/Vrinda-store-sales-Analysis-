# Vrinda Store Annual Sales Report 2022

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the annual sales performance of Vrinda Store for the year 2022 using Microsoft Excel. The objective of the analysis is to understand customer purchasing behavior, identify sales trends, and generate actionable insights to improve business performance in 2023.

---

## Objective

Vrinda Store aims to create an Annual Sales Report for 2022 to better understand customers and increase sales through data-driven decision-making.

---

---

## Key Business Questions

1. Compare sales and orders using a single chart.
2. Which month recorded the highest sales and orders?
3. Who purchased more in 2022 — men or women?
4. What were the different order statuses in 2022?
5. List the top 10 states contributing to sales.
6. Analyze the relationship between age and gender based on orders.
7. Which sales channel contributed the highest sales?
8. Which product category had the highest sales?

## Key Insights

* Women customers contributed approximately 64% of total sales.
* Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh were the top-performing states contributing nearly 35% of sales.
* Customers aged 30–49 years contributed around 50% of total sales.
* Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra generated approximately 80% of total sales.

---

## Final Conclusion

To improve sales performance in 2023, Vrinda Store should focus on women customers aged 30–49 years residing in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh through targeted advertisements, promotional offers, and discount campaigns on Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra.
